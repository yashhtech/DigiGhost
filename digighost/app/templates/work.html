What Does a Digital Marketer Do?

A digital marketer promotes products, services, or brands through online channels to drive traffic, engagement, and sales. They develop strategies for websites, social media, SEO, email, and paid ads while analyzing data to optimize performance. Their goal is to increase brand awareness and convert online users into customers.
